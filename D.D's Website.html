Our Mission

Is to provide people around the world with a flexible, personalized way to eat. We provide our customers a variety of innovative dishes from locally sourced ingredients (literally in the area!). We strive to give our consumers another food alternative without the hustle and bustle, queuing and waiting.

About Us

Everything you need to start the day right!

We know how it is! When you dont have the time, energy or inclination to have the most important meal of the day. Life just gets in the way. Order toasties, panninis, grilled sandwiches, bowls and more on you commute morning, noon, or night and feel satisfied throughout the day.

The Waiting is Over!

When you dont have the time to wait in the queue to order or wait for the not so sure's in front to decide. Order on the app and pick up for collection once youre nearby.

Get accurate time-location based notifications

Talk about Fast-Food! Order through the app from one of your pitshop enroute and get detailed countdowns to collection and notifications of how far you are from your nearest pitstop. Whether youre in a car, on the bus or train, or simply walking, pick up your order with no delays, fuss, or queuing.

Yours orders to suit your lifestyle :)

Trying to loose wait, bulk up, or just eat better (for a week or so!) Link the pitstop app with your health app and get details of your orders calories, or just order based on your strict dieting requirements its that simple.

Pool buy breakky!

Know people that have the same itch for great food first thing? Pool buy with colleagues, friends and family so everyone stays happy and fed!
