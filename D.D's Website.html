Our Mission. Its What We Do!

Pitstop provides people around the world with a flexible, personalized way to eat. The Waiting is Over!

When you dont have the time to wait in the queue to order or wait for the not so sure's in front to decide. Order on the app and pick up for collection once youre nearby.
